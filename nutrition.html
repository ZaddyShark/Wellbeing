NUTRITION CLINIC

Our Nutrition Clinic caters for individuals who want to have a comprehensive consultation to understand and improve their wellness by adopting healthier eating habits.

Our Nutrition Consultations include:

  • Holistic Wellness Consultancy: Health, Fitness, Nutrition ,& Mental Assessments with Wellness Report
  • Lifestyle Coaching Consultancy: Goal settings & Follow-up
  • Anthropometric Measurements Consultancy: BMI, WTH ratios, WC, Body fat etc.
  • Dietary Consultancy: History, Gap Analysis, Advice & Meal planning
  • Nutritional Supplementation Consultancy (Deficiency assessments & Advice)
  • IV Nutritional Therapy Consultation: Energy, Immunity, Rehydration & Iron - Drips and injections
