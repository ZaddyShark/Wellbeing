Who are we?

Wellbeing Academy is a 100% black female owned, BBBEE accredited company with over 15 years experience in wellness management and consultancy. We specialize in providing integrated wellness solutions for individuals and corporate groups focused on behaviour change

Why choose us?

At Wellbeing Academy we understand that wellness is at the core of our healthcare, and that “prevention is better and cheaper than cure” The world of healthcare has changed drastically over the years… “ Post-Covid epidemic effects”, “burnout syndromes” etc. have become a healthcare nightmare for many individuals. We also know that the current healthcare trends shows that the emotional , mental and physical wellbeing of an individual are intertwined , and there`s a need for a holistic approach when providing the wellness solutions for our clients
